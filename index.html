PUBLIC NOTICE ON THE FY 2026/
27 ANUAL DEVELOPMENT PLAN(ADP)

The county goverment of kajiado isin the process of preparing the fy 2026/27 Annual development plan ( ADP)pursuant to the provision of section 126 of the public finance management Act,2012.The constitution of Kenya,2010 provides for public participation in policy formulation.whereareas the county Goverment Act ,2012section 115 (1)and section 106()4 stipulates that public participation is madatory in county planing processes

In light of this,the county goverment of kajiado wishes to invite the general public,private sector organisation,civil society organisation,interersted groups yputh and all stake holders to attend the consultative meeting to be held on 20 august,2025 at masaai Technical Training institute,kajiado starting at 10:00am

The public are also encouraged to submit written memoranda/proposals to the CECM-the county treasury,county goverment of kajiado p.o.box 11-1101100 kajiado OR through the email:dbud@kajiado.go.ke,budget@gmail.com and copy to cdpokajiado@gmail.com

stakeholders are advised to align their memoranda/proposals with the development priorities set out in the 2023-2027 kajiado county intergrated development plan,which can be acccessed in the kajiado county website at www.kajiado.go.ke.
